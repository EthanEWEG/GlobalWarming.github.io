The Global Warming Catastrophe

Almost everyone has heard of climate change. It's a continuously evolving challenge we are facing around the world. Rising temperatures and earth’s increasing volatile climates have been key indicators for the ongoing shifts. The worst is only yet to come, yet no extensive efforts have been made in reducing the main culprits behind greenhouse gas emissions.

Greenhouse gas emissions are still on the rise globally. In fact, even if emissions were to flatline today it wouldn’t even be close to enough to prevent or slowdown the increase of volatile climates. The real solutions to start reducing c02 emissions require too much of a sacrifice for our quality and comfort that we’d rather let our world burn (sometimes quite literally). As the problem has become more and more mainstream fake solutions have become more and more popular as well, often in the form of “greenwashing”.

Two notable examples of fake solutions are:

  • Highly processed foods, wrapped in single use plastic, and packaged in single use cardboard boxes are placing "green" labels on their products such as “made with reusable plastics”. Which in reality is the complete wrong approach to getting rid of plastics
  • Canada telling their citizens to recycle but then actually shipping the waste to another country (usually China, Indonesia, or the Philippines) so they don’t have to build their own infrastructure to deal with the waste or face any consequences for dumping the garbage.
